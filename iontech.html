Drone...

A drone is an unmanned aircraft that is remotely controlled or flown autonomously through software-controlled flight plans.
They can be used for a variety of purposes, including aerial photography, surveillance, search and rescue, and military applications.
Modern drones are equipped with a variety of sensors and cameras, allowing them to take high-resolution images and videos from the air.
